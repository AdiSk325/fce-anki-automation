# Anki Check – Use of English Repair

## Kiedy zrobić

Zrób ten check po jednej albo dwóch rundach nauki talii Anki z dzisiejszej sesji.

## Cel

Sprawdzić, czy umiesz użyć materiału aktywnie, a nie tylko rozpoznać poprawną odpowiedź na karcie.

---

## Part A – Quick recall

Uzupełnij luki jednym słowem.

1. Many students are not aware ______ how much progress they can make in a month.

2. Regular revision contributes ______ greater confidence during the exam.

3. This is the kind ______ mistake that careful checking can catch.

4. Strong readers are sensitive ______ changes in tone and attitude.

---

## Part B – Active transformation

Use between two and five words, including the word given.

5. People think that the website is useful for exam practice.

THOUGHT

The website __________________ useful for exam practice.

6. It was not necessary for her to buy another workbook.

NEED

She __________________ another workbook.

7. I started revising phrasal verbs two months ago.

FOR

I have been revising phrasal verbs __________________.

8. I'm sorry that I didn't read the last paragraph carefully.

WISH

I __________________ the last paragraph carefully.

---

## Part C – Transfer

Napisz po angielsku 4 własne zdania:

- jedno z `aware of`
- jedno z `contribute to`
- jedno z `kind of`
- jedno z `I wish I had ...`

Jeśli chcesz, odeślij ten plik z odpowiedziami do sprawdzenia.

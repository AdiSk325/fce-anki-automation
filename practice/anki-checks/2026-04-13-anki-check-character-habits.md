# Anki Check – Character And Habits

## Kiedy zrobić

Zrób ten check po nauczeniu się nowych kart z talii `fce-vocabulary-character-habits.tsv`.

## Cel

Sprawdzić, czy umiesz nie tylko rozpoznać słownictwo, ale też aktywnie go użyć w kontekście FCE.

---

## Part A – Gap fill

Uzupełnij luki jednym lub dwoma słowami.

1. It would be ______ to believe that every manager always accepts criticism calmly.

2. She is very ______ and refuses to change her opinion even when everyone disagrees.

3. If you want to improve your English, you need to make an ______ every day.

4. Good study routines should not get in the way ______ your normal life.

5. I'm really looking forward ______ using these expressions in my speaking exam.

6. After a few weeks, most people can adapt ______ a new habit.

---

## Part B – Choose the best word

Wybierz najlepsze słowo.

7. Someone who believes good things will happen is:

a) arrogant
b) optimistic
c) stubborn

8. Someone who you can always trust and depend on is:

a) loyal
b) naive
c) ambitious

9. A person who strongly wants success is:

a) sensitive
b) respected
c) ambitious

10. A person who makes others feel excited and motivated is:

a) inspiring
b) self-confident
c) arrogant

---

## Part C – Accuracy repair

Popraw błędy.

11. He is very passionated about science and research.

12. You can always rely her when things go wrong.

13. She is determined of passing the exam this year.

14. I look forward to go to university next year.

## Part D – Transfer

Napisz po angielsku 6 własnych zdań. Użyj w nich:

- `self-confident`
- `motivated`
- `give up`
- `take time out`
- `do well in`
- `get in the way of`

Jeśli chcesz, odeślij odpowiedzi do sprawdzenia.

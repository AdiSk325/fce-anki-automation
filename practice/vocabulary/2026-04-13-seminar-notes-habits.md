# Seminar Notes Vocabulary

Source vocabulary and useful phrases from the attached seminar notes about habits and change.

| Word / phrase | English definition | Polish translation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| habit | something you do regularly, often without thinking much about it | nawyk |
| adapt to | to change your behavior or thinking so that you can deal with a new situation | przystosowac sie do |
| give up | to stop doing something, especially a habit or activity | porzucic; przestac |
| get in the way of | to create a problem or prevent something from happening easily | przeszkadzac w |
| take time out | to stop your usual activities for a period of rest or to do something else | zrobic sobie przerwe |
| make an effort | to try hard to do something | postarac sie; wlozyc wysilek |
| seminar | a class or meeting in which people discuss a subject with a teacher or expert | seminarium |
| change | the process of becoming different or making something different | zmiana |
| knit | to make clothes or other things from wool using two long needles or a machine | robic na drutach; dziergac |
| research (v.) | to study a subject carefully in order to discover new facts or information | badac; prowadzic badania |
| research (n.) | careful study of a subject to discover new facts or information | badania |

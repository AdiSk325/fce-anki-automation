# Character Adjectives Notes 2

Source vocabulary from the attached exercise about character adjectives.

| Word | English definition | Polish translation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| respected | admired and considered important or good by other people | szanowany |
| inspiring | making people feel excited, encouraged, or full of new ideas | inspirujacy |
| determined | having a clear decision and not letting difficulties stop you | zdeterminowany |
| motivated | very willing to work hard or make an effort to achieve something | zmotywowany |
| stubborn | unwilling to change your opinion or behavior, even when it would be better to do so | uparty |

## Quick contrast

- `determined` is positive and focuses on strong purpose.
- `stubborn` is usually negative and focuses on refusing to change.

# Character Adjectives Usage Notes

Source vocabulary and useful phrases from the attached exercise with example sentences.

| Word / phrase | English definition | Polish translation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| sensitive | easily hurt or upset by criticism or what people say | wrazliwy |
| self-confident | feeling sure of yourself and your abilities | pewny siebie |
| optimistic | expecting good things to happen | optymistyczny |
| arrogant | behaving as if you are better or more important than others | arogancki |
| loyal | always supporting someone and staying faithful to them | lojalny |
| passionate | having very strong positive feelings about something | pelen pasji |
| ambitious | wanting very much to be successful | ambitny |
| naive | too willing to believe that people are always honest or kind | naiwny |
| rely on | to trust or depend on someone or something | polegac na |
| look forward to | to feel happy and excited about something that is going to happen | czekac z niecierpliwoscia na |
| do well in | to be successful in a subject, activity, or situation | radzic sobie dobrze w |
| negative feedback | criticism or comments that show what is wrong or needs improvement | negatywna informacja zwrotna |

## Useful examples

- `rely on her`
- `look forward to going to work`
- `do well in her career`
- `be sensitive to negative feedback`
